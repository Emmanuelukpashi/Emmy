About Benue Tech Skills

Benue Tech Skills abreviated as BTS is a digital skill acquisitions
project sponsores by the Governor of Beue State,
His Excellency Rev. Fr. Dr.Hyacinth Iormem Alia. the aim of theproject is to
place the Benue youth into global competitivenes in digital skills.
The project is aimed to train 10,000 youths in the state.
